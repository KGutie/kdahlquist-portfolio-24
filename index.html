Welcome!

Kristy Dahlquist, PMP

Program Manager

Hey there! I’m a full-stack problem solver who brings teams, ideas, and systems together to make great things happen. Whether I’m building programs, strategies, workflows, experiences, or even the perfect sandwich, I thrive on creating structure that fuels creativity and turns ideas into reality.

I’ve worked with customer-obsessed brands like REI, Okta, and Auth0, leading marketing and program management efforts that connect developers, customers, and communities in meaningful ways. One day I might be crafting a high-impact campaign. The next I’m streamlining a process, automating a workflow, or (fun fact!) coding this very portfolio from scratch in HTML and CSS. I love fast-moving environments where I can solve problems, build smarter systems, and bring people together to get things done.

If you’re looking for someone who brings energy, adaptability, and a people-first approach to every challenge, let’s connect on LinkedIn!
